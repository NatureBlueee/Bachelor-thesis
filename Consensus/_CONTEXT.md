## 论文主题

**AI素养对Z世代员工向上影响行为的影响：资源依赖视角**

The Impact of AI Literacy on Upward Influence Behavior among Generation Z Employees: A Resource Dependence Perspective

## 核心理论框架

### 主理论：资源依赖理论 (Resource Dependence Theory)
- **来源**: Pfeffer & Salancik (1978)
- **核心观点**: 权力来源于对稀缺、有价值资源的控制
- **应用**: Tripathi (2021) 将RDT扩展到员工-上级关系，员工作为"tacit resources的载体"

### 辅助理论
- **社会认知理论** (Bandura, 1997): 解释感知资源优势如何转化为行为
- **领导建构理论** (DeRue & Ashford, 2010): 解释claiming-granting动态

## 关键变量

| 变量 | 类型 | 定义 | 测量方式 |
|------|------|------|----------|
| **AI素养** | 自变量 | AI知识 + AI情感 + AI思维 (钟柏昌, 2024) | 访谈探索 |
| **感知资源优势** | 中介变量 | 员工对自己持有上级需要但缺乏的AI资源的主观认知 | 访谈探索 |
| **向上影响行为** | 因变量 | 想法生成、想法推广、想法实施 (参考Scott & Bruce, 1994) | 访谈探索 |
| **上级开放性** | 调节变量 | 上级对下属意见和新技术的接受程度 (Detert & Burris, 2007) | 访谈探索 |

## 研究命题

- **P1**: AI素养 → 感知资源优势
- **P2**: 感知资源优势 → 向上影响行为
- **P3**: 感知资源优势中介AI素养与向上影响行为的关系
- **P4**: 上级开放性调节P2的关系

## 提问上下文模板（复制使用）

```
I am conducting a qualitative study on how AI literacy enables Generation Z employees to engage in upward influence behavior in Chinese organizations. 

My theoretical framework is based on Resource Dependence Theory (Pfeffer & Salancik, 1978; Tripathi, 2021), examining how AI literacy creates a resource-based power that enables employees to influence their supervisors.

Key constructs:
- AI Literacy: AI knowledge, AI affectivity, AI thinking (Zhong et al., 2024)
- Perceived Resource Advantage: Employees' subjective perception of holding AI-related resources their supervisors need but lack
- Upward Influence Behavior: Idea generation, promotion, and implementation
- Supervisor Openness: The extent to which supervisors are receptive to subordinate input (moderator)

Currently, I have completed the literature review and methodology chapters, and I am preparing for data collection (15-20 interviews with Gen Z knowledge workers).
```
